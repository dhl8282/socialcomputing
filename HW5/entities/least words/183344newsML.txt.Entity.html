Leading data networking stocks settled back for a round of profit-taking Tuesday after the strong price gains of recent weeks, securities analysts said. After opening at a 52-week high of 67, industry flagship Cisco Systems Inc retreated throughout the day and was trading off 1-3/8 to 65-1/8. Similarly, Cabletron Systems Inc and 3Com Corp began to fall immediately after Tuesday's opening. By mid-afternon, Cabletron had lost 1-1/8 to 67-3/4, while 3Com was down 2 to 70-1/8.
